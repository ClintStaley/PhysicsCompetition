STEM Competitions

This website provides educational STEM design competitions. You create a team (including teams of one if you want to work individually) for a competition, and submit a design. The site determines if your design is correct, and either gives you hints on what needs fixing, or simulates your design on the problem and gives it a quality score.

Simulations

Realistic simulation of each design problem, and of your solution to it, is a major feature of the site. Whether it's a gravitational bounce challenge, an elastic-collisions cannon, or a bridge design, you get both a schematic movie of your design at work, and a CGI 3-D movie of it (with VR enhancement if you have VR goggles).
